Product Support

For technical issues contact support@relisoft.com . We will try to get back to you as soon as possible, usually within a few hours.

Please provide as much information as possible about your setup and the circumstances leading to the problem. Try running diagnostics or Project>Repair. Read the Frequently Asked Questions.

Create a diagnostic file using Help>Save Diagnostics and attach it to your report.


Alternatively, you can visit our online forum and participate in discussions with us and other developers. Support questions are answered in the forum promptly.
