Restoring old Versions of the Project

You can send the whole project back in time to any of the previous versions stored in History, whether it's just undoing the last check-in or one from a couple weeks or months back..


To restore a project

  • Select the version in History you wish to undo and choose Selection > Restore.
    • If History is filtered, only the selected files will be restored.
    • Files that haven't changed since the selected version are not restored—there's nothing to restore!
  • The restored files will be listed in the Check-in Area. You can review the results of the restore by double-clicking the files. You can also make additional changes.
  • To propogate the restore to others you must check-in the files. Or, you can abandon the restore by doing an Un-Checkout of the files

Incidentally, if you change your mind later, you can still undo the restoration by restoring the version before your restoration.

You can also restore individual files by selecting them in the lower pane and selecting Restore File Version

from the Selection menu.
