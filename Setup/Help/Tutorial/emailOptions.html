Email Options

In some situations, especially during the distribution of full sync scripts, you will need to split a large script up into smaller chunks in order to send it over e-mail. Your ISP or IT department can tell you the maximum attachment size they support. By default Code Co-op sets it to 1024Kb. Here the dispatcher is set to split scripts larger than 2000Kb.
