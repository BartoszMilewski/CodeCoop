How do I rename my computer?


In order to rename your computer, you'll have to change the forwarding path to your computer.

  • If the computer a satellite, you will do the following:
    • Open the Dispatcher by double-clicking the icon in the task bar
    • Select Program > Collaboration Settings
    • From the Network Satellite Tab, you will edit "This satellites Public Inbox share"
    • Click the Advanced button below this text and make your change
  • The change will be propagated to the Hub.
    • To double check the change, open the dispatcher on the Hub and select the Members tab. Double-click on a project that you are part of and verify your forwarding path.
  • If the computer is a Hub, you will do the following:
    • Open the Dispatcher by double-clicking the icon in the task bar
    • Select Program > Collaboration Settings
    • From the Network Hub tab, you will edit "This Hub's public inbox share"
    • Click the Advanced button below this text and make your change
  • The change will be propagated to the satellites.
    • To double check the change, open the dispatcher on a satellite machine. Select Collaboration Settings. Verify that the Hub's Public Inbox has changed.
